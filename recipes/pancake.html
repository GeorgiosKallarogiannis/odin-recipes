Pancake recipe

Description

This is a bulk pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make! It also makes good waffles.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; store in an airtight container.
